Commonly Asked Questions

What is Sameday?

Sameday™ is an express design as a service platform specialized in delivering high-quality design briefs on the same day. We offer both one-off and subscription-based pricing options to suit your design needs.

What is the difference between the one-off and subscription pricing models?
  • One-off Pricing: With our one-off pricing, you pay per design brief. This option is ideal for occasional design needs without a long-term commitment.
  • Subscription Pricing: Our subscription plans offer discounted rates for ongoing design needs. You can choose between the Pro or Enterprise subscription plans. Sameday is dedicated to providing you with consistent and cost-effective design solutions at record-breaking turnaround times.
What is a project board?

A project board is a collaborative workspace where team members can manage, track, and organize tasks and projects.

It serves as a central hub for project management, allowing team members to visualize the progress of tasks, assign responsibilities, set deadlines, and communicate effectively.

At Sameday™, we use Telegram for this purpose. 

Why don't I just hire a full-time designer?

Hiring a full-time designer might seem like the obvious choice, but the reality is that finding a truly exceptional senior-level designer is a massive challenge. With the high demand for experienced designers, securing top-tier talent can be a daunting task.

Instead of facing these hurdles, consider leveraging the assurance that Sameday™ offers you.

With a dedicated focus on ultrafast turnaround while maintaining the integrity and accuracy of design executions, we can reliably provide high-quality, tailored solutions without the long-term commitment and overhead of a full-time hire. 

How long will it take to receive my designs?

All design requests sent on or before 10A.M (WAT) will be treated on the Sameday. Requests that are later than 10 A.M (WAT) may carry over to the next day. 

Do I have access to the raw files? My printer may need it.

Yes. All assets fully belong to you. If you ever need your design source files, do not hesitate to request them.

Who are the designers and developers?

We have on stand-by, over 1,200 highly experienced graphic designers, WordPress web developers, and content writers, with each playing different roles to ensure that the unique selling proposition of Sameday™ is unconditionally fulfilled.

Is there any design work you don't cover?

All the design works we do have already been outlined. Any type of design work not mentioned on this website will not be covered under any of our plans. These include, but are not limited to: Complex multi-paged websites with more than 5 pages, Complex multipaged e-commerce websites with more than 8 pages, 3D modeling, CAD drawings, animated graphics (GIFS, etc.), document design (medical forms, etc.), complex packaging designs, and extensive print design (magazines, books, etc.).

Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?
We invest an enormous amount of money and manhours towards ensuring that end-products are of maximum quality without losing track of deadlines. Due to this, it will be practically impossible to issue refunds. However, you may still take advantage of the free additional revisions provided by us.
Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

There are no limits. You can make as many request in a day as possible. However, bear in mind that being a one-off plan, you would have to pay for every task or project to be completed.

How do I know which one-off plan to choose?

There is no best plan. What you choose depends on the nature of your design request. 

The applicable category of request have been detailed under each one-off plan. Please refer to the #pricing section for detailed information on this.

How do I request designs?

Simply scroll to the pricing section and determine which of the three one-off plans is the best for you based on the category of your task or project. Click on the “Choose” button and follow the prompt. 

Will I also get a project board for my design requests?

Yes, you will get a Telegram project channel (We love to call this “Project Board”) for your design request. We use this Telegram feature as a project board. 

We use Telegram because chats and media files shared in the channel/group are forever available and accross multiple devices.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

There are no limits. You can enque and prioritize as many design requests as possible using Listmoz (https://listmoz.com/), but you are only guaranteed one execution per day on the Pro plan and two executions per day on the Enterprise plan. 

See an example: https://listmoz.com/#XRN3XrHny05zJtspDB6r

How do I follow up on Tasks being carried out

Each project will have its own “space” on Telegram. This can foster the collection of feedback and makes other forms of correspondence easier.

Why use Telegram instead of WhatsApp, Slack or Asana?

We have singled out the use of Telegram Channels as our project board mechanism due to the following reasons:

  1. Messages and attachments are forever available.
  2. Telegram is accessible from multiple devices.
  3. It is easily accessible and doesn’t have a steep learning curve like dedicated project management apps like Asana, Slack, and Trello.
Which subscription plan is better?

Yes, there are two types of plans under the subscription model namely:

  • Pro 
  • Enterprise

The major difference between the two subscription options is that the Enterprise plan supports two concurrent tasks/projects, whereas the Pro plan only supports one concurrent task or project.

Can I switch between plans or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. However, changes to your plan will take effect at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

How do I request designs?

If you are subscribed under one of the two subscription plans, a special Telegram group would be created for you for recurring requests.

You can request designs by simply dropping instructions/brief in that Telegram group. 

You can enque and order design briefs in order of priority with the help of Listmoz.
